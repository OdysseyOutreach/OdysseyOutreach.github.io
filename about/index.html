Services

In Odyssey Outreach we offer a multiple of educational services to participating public education local communities surrounding UNC-Charlotte:

  • Special free-of-charge tutoring to AP Computer Science Students (On-Campus and Online)
  • Weekly On-Campus Sessions to supplement the AP Computer Science Curriculum
  • Year-round Computing Events on High School campuses
  • Connecting High Schoolers with College life.
  • Participate in curriculum nights.
